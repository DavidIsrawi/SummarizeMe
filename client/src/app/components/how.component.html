Summarize me

Summarize any body of text you want by 70-80%! Just plug it into the text box and submit it.

How it works

  • Removes words with no value from the article (e.g. 'a', 'to', 'am')
  • Find most meaningful words
  • Assign value to each sentence depending on the words it contains
  • Build summary depending on the value of all sentences and the outstanding ones
